Social Media

What is social media?

Social media has been serving people around the globe for years. It helped people regardless the race, age, status in life, and needs. In studies, work, and even personal needs and wants, it had contributed a lot. After all, it would not had been invented if it will serve no purpose.


So what is really the purpose of social media?

To start off, i have to say it has a lot of purposes. It serves people in many different ways depending on their needs and wants. One of its purposes is communication. It had helped people connect to each other especially friends and families who are distant from each other.

Another is finding new friends. It is no doubt that there a lot of people that are very extroverted and love meeting new people and earn more friends.

Lastly, through social media, you will be able to share anything your mind can think of. You show the world your talent, skills, stories, and life. Basically, you are free to do anything on social media. But so are those with bad intentions. There are people who abuse the purpose of social media, so here comes cyber bullying and cyber crimes. Yes, we all know that social media is really helpful, but let's not abuse its power.

Social media has many uses. It helps us communicate with our friends and family. It has its pros and cons but we nowadays chose to ignore it. Here, the disadvantages will be listed.

Social media can be a source of cyberbullying. It is also one of the reasons why teenagers commit suicide. It can also facilitate laziness and reduce family time. It can make or break us, the young or the old. We should always be careful when using social media.

As we all know that social media has a big role in our life. It makes our tasks easier. It is also helpful in many ways. It also helps us in our researches. It also help us in our communication with our loved ones.Social media is the fastest way to communicate with our loved ones who are in faraway places. It is also convenient and easy to use.But we should always be reminded not to abuse these.

about
